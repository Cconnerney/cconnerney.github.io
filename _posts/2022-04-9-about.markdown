<h2 id="About-Me">About Me<a class="anchor-link" href="#About-Me">&#182;</a></h2><p>Hi my name is Cristin, thanks for visiting my site. I'm a data science student at Carnegie Mellon passionate about machine learning, automation, data analysis, visualization, and research. Recently I have applied my interests to web development, and creating this website as a fun side project.</p>
<p>In my former life I competed for the USA Gymnastics national team, traveling the world to represent the United States. I reached the senior elite level and traveled to China, Poland, Switzerland, Germany, Italy, and throughout the US. I performed on <a href="https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=SH-XyQ9e308">America's got Talent</a> (placing 3rd) and at the US Olympic Trials. Acrobatics taught me that no goal was out of reach, and provided a roadmap for success in academic endeavors.</p>
